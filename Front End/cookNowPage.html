Spaghetti Carbonara

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

  • Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  • Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  • In a separate bowl, mix eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  • In a skillet, cook chopped bacon until crispy.
  • Drain cooked spaghetti and add it to the skillet with the bacon.
  • Turn off the heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture, allowing the residual heat to cook the eggs without scrambling.
  • Serve hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley if desired.
